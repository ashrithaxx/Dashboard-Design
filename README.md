# Dashboard Design
## Task 4

## Dataset
- Superstore Sales Dataset

## Tools Used
- Power BI

## Visualizations Created
- Total Sales, Profit, Quantity, and Profit Margin (KPI Cards)
- Sales Trend Over Time
- Sales by Category
- Profit by Region
- Sales by Customer Segment
- Interactive Filters (Region, Category, Order Date)

## Insights
- West region generates the highest profit.
-	Sales show a steady increase from 2015 to 2017.
-	Technology category has the highest sales.
-	Consumer segment contributes the largest share of sales.

## Business Recommendations
-	Focus marketing on high-performing regions like West.
-	Invest more in Technology products.
-	Improve performance in low profit regions.
-	Target the Consumer segment for growth.
